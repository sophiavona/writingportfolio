About

I am a writer and researcher interested in the intersection of literature, science, and public health. My work explores themes of interdisciplinarity, antimicrobial resistance, and the power of narrative in scientific communication.

Currently studying at UNC Chapel Hill, I have been published in various literary magazines and have presented research at academic conferences.
